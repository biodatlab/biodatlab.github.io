# Monrada Chiewhawan

Monrada Chiewhawan is a dual-degree student pursuing the Doctor of Medicine and Master of Engineering (Biomedical Engineering) at the Faculty of Medicine Ramathibodi Hospital and the Department of Biomedical Engineering, Mahidol University.

She is a passionate learner driven by the goal of advancing AI for science and medicine, with recent work spanning clinical NLP, medical imaging, and scientific figure generation.
