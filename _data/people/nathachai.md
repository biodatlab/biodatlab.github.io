Nathachai Rathamarit is an undergraduate student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Mahidol University, specializing in machine learning for healthcare applications.

Leveraging his proficiency in Python and C/C++, he aims to bridge the gap between computational physics and medical innovation. With a strong academic foundation, Nathachai previously represented Thailand in the Sakura Science High School Exchange Program in Japan and was a Gold Medalist at the Thailand Young Physicists' Tournament (TYPT).

His current research interests focus on Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) and utilizing deep learning techniques to advance clinical solutions.
